BICOL EXPRESS

₱150

Description

Bicol Express is a spicy pork dish simmered in coconut milk, shrimp paste, and lots of chili peppers. It’s creamy, spicy, and bold in flavor.

Why order it in a restaurant:

  • The heat level can be customized by chefs to suit your taste.
  • Some ingredients like shrimp paste (bagoong) can be tricky to cook at home.
  • Great for adventurous eaters looking to spice up their meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, sliced into small pieces
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 5-8 red chili peppers (siling labuyo), chopped
  • 2 long green chilies (siling haba), sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, then sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in shrimp paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Cook for about 20 minutes until pork is tender.
  5. Add chili peppers and coconut cream, then simmer for another 5-10 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
